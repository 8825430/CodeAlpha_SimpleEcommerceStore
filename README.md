### About Page

**Headline:** Started With One Bad Cup of Coffee

Fernway began the way a lot of good ideas do — out of frustration. Our founder couldn't find a coffee shop in the neighborhood that treated coffee like the craft it is. So in 2019, Fernway opened with a tiny roaster in the back and a mission up front: source honestly, roast carefully, and never rush the pour.

Today we work directly with farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, rotating our single-origin offerings seasonally so you're always tasting something at its peak.

**Our values**
- **Traceable sourcing.** We know the name of every farm we buy from.
- **Small-batch roasting.** Never more than we can sell fresh that week.
- **Community first.** This is your third place — stay as long as you like.
